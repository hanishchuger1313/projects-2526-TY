Here’s a concise roundup of the latest developments in education:
 Key Updates in Education
- NCERT Under Scrutiny: The NCERT faced criticism after the Supreme Court flagged errors in a textbook chapter. The council has since apologized, calling the mistake “purely unintentional.” This has reignited debates about ideological bias in educational material.
- Vocational Courses in Schools: In Madhya Pradesh, new vocational programs are raising concerns that they may reinforce gender stereotypes rather than break them.
- AI and Education: Experts argue that universities must adapt quickly to the rise of AI, treating education as an evolving “operating system” rather than a static model.
